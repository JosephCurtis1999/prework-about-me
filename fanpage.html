Bio

Avelino, hailing from North London, is arguably the greatest lyricist coming from the UK. His slick bars separate him from a crowd of rappers who rely on autotune and generic lyrics. In a recent freestyle he can be quoted saying "They call me silver surfer because they marvel at me flowing". This is a reference to the way the silver surfer flows through the air on his surfboard, referencing how his delivery on a beat is so smooth based on the recognition he receives from fans and listeners.
